Test Your Knowledge:
GIE Tutorial Quiz

Assess your understanding of geospatial impact evaluation concepts, data sources, analytical methods, and best practices covered in the tutorial modules. This 20-question quiz will help you identify areas of mastery and topics that may benefit from additional review.

Quiz Sections
Section 1: GIE Fundamentals (Questions 1–5): Test your understanding of what GIE is, how it differs from traditional methods, and when to apply it.
Section 2: Spatial Data and Remote Sensing (Questions 6–10): Assess your knowledge of satellite platforms, NDVI, Google Earth Engine, and georeferenced survey data.
Section 3: Analysis Methods and Causal Inference (Questions 11–15): Evaluate your grasp of difference-in-differences, matching, regression discontinuity, and spatial spillovers.
Section 4: Application and Best Practices (Questions 16–20): Apply concepts to real-world scenarios and identify common pitfalls and quality assurance practices.
Scoring Guide
18–20 correct (90–100%): Excellent mastery. You're ready to apply GIE methods.
15–17 correct (75–85%): Good understanding. Review specific areas before independent analysis.
12–14 correct (60–70%): Satisfactory foundation. Consider the full AidData GIE Toolkit.
Below 12 (< 60%): Additional study required. Re-watch modules and explore resources.
Completion Certificate
Score 80% or higher (16+ correct answers) to receive an official AidData certificate of completion. Your certificate will include your name, completion date, and score, verifying your proficiency in GIE fundamentals, spatial data analysis, and evaluation methods.
