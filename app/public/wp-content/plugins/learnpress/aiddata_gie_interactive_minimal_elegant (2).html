AidData

Learning from space

Satellites track development outcomes. Rigorous methods turn observations into evidence. AidData bridges the two.

01

Tiny roots, big impacts

Vitamin-A rich sweetpotato spreads through Tanzania, Ethiopia, and Ghana. Satellites measure adoption. Surveys explain why—who adopts, where barriers exist, how information travels through communities.

02

Who benefits?

Performance depends on access. Gendered communication pathways shape who receives training, inputs, and peer support. Evaluation must ask not just "did it work?" but "who was reached?"

03

From methods to practice

Earth observation improves evaluation when it strengthens measurement, coverage, or timeliness—without weakening validity. The goal: better monitoring, faster learning, clearer decisions.
